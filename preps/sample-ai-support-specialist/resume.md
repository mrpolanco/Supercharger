# Sample Resume: Jordan Lee

Jordan Lee is a fictional candidate used to demonstrate Supercharger's prep
workflow.

## Summary

Technical support engineer with three years of experience supporting developer
tools and SaaS integrations. Comfortable reproducing customer issues with curl,
reading API docs, querying support data with SQL, and writing clear escalation
notes for engineering.
